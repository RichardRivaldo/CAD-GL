Drawing

Choose the Draw mode in the Input Modes dropdown.

Choose the shape you want to draw in Shape Types dropdown.

For line, just click on the canvas twice. For polygons, click on the canvas as many as the number of edges of the polygon. Else, just click once on the canvas, and you can also change the initial size of the object.

Resizing

Choose the Resize mode.

Select the object you want to resize, and adjust the size by the Size Scaler slider

Moving Vertex

Choose the Move Vertex mode.

Select one vertex you want to move, and move the cursor to the desired position.

Click on the target position to apply the changes.

Filling Color

Choose the Fill Color mode and choose any color in Shape Colors dropdown.

Select the object you want to apply the color on.

Saving and Loading Models

Click either one of the button, and then just do steps as saving or uploading files as usual (JSON format).
